Welcome to High or Low

A simple card game where you guess if the next card will be higher or lower

Start with $100, place your bets, and see how much you can win!

Play Game

How to Play

It's pretty simple, just follow these steps:

1. Place Your Bet

Type in how much you want to bet or use the quick buttons (+$10, +$100, +$1000, MAX).

2. First Card Shows Up

Click "Place Bet" and the first card appears. This is your reference card.

3. Make Your Guess

Click High, Low, or Same. High means the next card will be higher, Low means lower, Same means same value.

4. See What Happens

If you're right, your bet gets multiplied by the odds shown. If you're wrong, you lose everything.

5. Cash Out or Keep Going

After winning, you can cash out to add money to your balance, or keep playing with your winnings.

6. Game Over on Wrong Guess

Guess wrong and you lose your bet. Start over with a new deck!

Game Rules

Here's what you need to know:

Starting Balance

Everyone starts with $100. Don't bet more than you have!

Fresh Deck Every Game

Each game uses a new shuffled 52-card deck. No tricks here!

Card Values

Ace = 1, 2-10 = face value, Jack = 11, Queen = 12, King = 13. Suits don't matter.

Betting

Minimum bet is > 0. Maximum is whatever you have. Use the buttons or type your own amount.

Ready to Play?

Now that you know how it works, give it a try!
